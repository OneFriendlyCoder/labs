What to practise

  • Fixed width of the site wrapper: identify and modify the fixed width to see effects.
  • Liquid sections: hero and footer stretch full width — try changing their padding and inner max-width.
  • Flexbox: modify the filter bar and nav to experiment alignment and wrapping.
  • Grid: change columns in the gallery (.market-grid) — experiment with minmax() and auto-fit.
  • Responsive: use DevTools to test breakpoints included in the CSS.
